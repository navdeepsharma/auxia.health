How It Works

Simple enough to actually do.
Smart enough to actually work.

01

Check in with your AI coach

Each session starts with a 10-second mood and sleep check-in. Your AI coach uses this to select today's 4 activities and calibrate difficulty — so every session is right for where you are today.

02

Train across 4 cognitive domains

Working memory, processing speed, attention, and language — the four domains most strongly linked to cognitive reserve. Each domain gets one adaptive game per session, drawn from a rotating library so your brain never adapts and stops growing.

03

Track your progress over time

Your Insights screen tracks every domain, shows your 30-day activity calendar, identifies your peak performance day, and generates a monthly AI health report you can share with your GP.

The Science

Built on research,
not promises.

Auxia is informed by decades of cognitive neuroscience and the landmark ACTIVE Trial — the most rigorous long-term study of cognitive training ever conducted.

29%
Dementia risk reduction
The ACTIVE Trial 20-year follow-up found that speed-of-processing training may reduce dementia risk by up to 29% — the strongest result in the field to date.
20yr
Longitudinal evidence
Participants were followed for two decades. The cognitive benefits of consistent training persisted long after the training period ended.
Now
The best time to start
Brain changes linked to Alzheimer's begin 15–20 years before symptoms appear. The earlier you build cognitive reserve, the greater the protection — but it's never too early or too late.
